Information on the Rock Edition
  • This version contains every Rock-type Pokédex entry on Global PokédeX Plus.
  • To mark a Pokémon as "owned" you can use the JavaScript feature by left-clicking on it's icon.
  • Hover over icons to see their Pokédex number, species name, current form and typing.
  • National Pokédex order takes priority in the sorting of entries, including Novelty Pokémon.
  • Pokémon that haven't been released use placeholder data, which will be updated in the future.

Stuck and need some help? You can always contact Rookie Roo for clarifications or updates!
